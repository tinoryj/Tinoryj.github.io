Biography

Yanjing Ren is a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Patrick P. C. Lee. He is also a member of the Applied Distributed Systems Lab (ADSLab) at CUHK. Before that, He received his M.S degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2022, where he worked with Prof. Jingwei Li at the Network Security Technology Lab (NTSL), which Prof. Xiaosong Zhang leads. In addition, He received his Bachelor's degree in Information Security from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2019.
